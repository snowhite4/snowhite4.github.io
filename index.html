Myer-brigg Personality Test


The Architect

Strengths

The strengths of the architect primarily centre aroung cognitive response and approach to solving problems, one such strength is being strongly independent. Another strength of the architect is being easy going and able to adapt they also are confident in their skills and approach new tasks with out of the box solutions.

Weaknesses

Weaknesses of the Architect revolve around feeling mainly the lack thereof, Architect's have problem making decisions based on feelings and don't understand the importance of such decisions. Another weakness is social interaction, they feel the need to keep their work and idea's to themselves, they tend to place high expectations on themselves and others around, so much so they seem critical towards others.
